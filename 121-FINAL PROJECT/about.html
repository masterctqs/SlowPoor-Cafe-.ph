About SlowPoor Cafe

More than a cup. It's a ritual.

Who We Are

SlowPoor began as a small idea between two friends who loved coffee. We believe every cup tells a story, from the farmers who grow the beans to the baristas who prepare them.

We opened our doors in 2026 with a simple promise: make good coffee and take the time to do it right.

Today our cafe has become a place where people relax, study, and meet friends while enjoying quality coffee.

Our Values

  • Craft FirstEvery drink is made carefully.
  • Ethical SourcingWe support coffee farmers.
  • CommunityOur cafe is a place for everyone.
  • SustainabilityWe try to reduce waste.
