Our Story

More Than a Cup of Coffee

SlowPoor began as a small idea between two friends who loved coffee. We believe every cup tells a story — from the farmers who grow the beans to the baristas who prepare them.

We opened our doors in 2026 with a simple promise: make good coffee and take the time to do it right.
