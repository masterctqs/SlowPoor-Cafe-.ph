Allergen Information

Items on our menu may contain the following allergens. Please inform our staff of any dietary requirements.

  • Gluten (Croissant, Flat Bread, Lasagna, Tiramisu Cake)

  • Eggs (Lasagna, Cheesecake, Tiramisu Cake)
  • Nuts (Overnight Oats – optional toppings)
  • Soy (Matcha Latte – if oat milk substituted)
  • Gluten-free options available on request
  • Vegan-friendly: Hibiscus, Lemonade, Overnight Oats (base)
  • All items prepared in a shared kitchen
